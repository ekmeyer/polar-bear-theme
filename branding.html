Branding

During my time at Lockton, we united Lockton under one brand globablly. It was great experience getting to work with a team of webmasters, designers and public relations specialists to launch the global brand. To share our new identity, we released [a fresh global website](https://lockton.com), [a catchy new tagline](https://vimeo.com/370952932) and an overall look and feel that demonstrates Lockton's story - past, present and future.
