Well, hello!

I’m Erin, a St. Louis native currently living in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.

I graduated from the University of Kansas in May 2016 with a degree in strategic communications, a journalism degree with a creative twist, and a minor in business.

In August 2016, I traded my toasted ravioli and Cardinals baseball for the mountains and craft beer when I accepted a job in the athletic communications at Wake Forest. My job perfectly combines three of my favorite things: sports, social media and all things comms!

Expanding on my current position, I am an Assistant Director of Athletic Communications at Wake Forest after starting there as an intern. At Wake Forest, I update and manage content on wakeforestsports.com, create graphics for various teams, keep and maintain stats and records, produce game notes and serve as the liaison between student-athletes and the media. Since I began in August 2016, I have worked with the field hockey, volleyball, women’s soccer, men’s and women’s basketball, cross country and track & field programs.

Previously, I interned for the for the World Champion Kansas City Royals in the Communications and Media Relations department from April – August 2016. I helped create postgame notes, minor league reports and stat packs while managing the press box on game days.

My senior year, I interned in the University of Kansas Athletics Communications Department. My main focus was in sports information for the men’s basketball team. Through this position, I worked on many events including the Big 12 Men’s Basketball Tournament in Kansas City, Mo., the first and second rounds of the NCAA Women’s Volleyball Tournament, the NCAA Track & Field West Preliminary Round and more.

I’ve experienced so much more that can be found throughout this site!

I’m fascinated by social media and pop culture, especially surrounding sports. When I’m not doing anything work related, I love to bake and one day hope to open a bakery. I enjoy drinking ridiculous amounts of coffee, playing with puppies and goofing off in InDesign.

So, let’s talk! I can be contacted by email here or through my LinkedIn page. Resume, additional work samples and references are available upon request.

Rock Chalk,
Erin
